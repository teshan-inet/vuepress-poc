Login

This section explains how to login and access your INET Gateway account securely and successfully.

Prerequisites

Before logging in, ensure you have:

  • A registered user account for Gateway
  • Your email
  • Your password
  • A stable internet connection

If you do not have an account, please contact your administrator.

Accessing the Login Page

  1. Open your browser.
  2. Enter the Gateway URL provided by your administrator.
  3. Press Enter.
  4. You will be redirected to the Login Page.

Login Page – Initial View

Login Page – I

The Login Page contains the following components:

  • Email address field – Enter your registered email.
  • Submit Button – Click to proceed further.

Once the email address has been entered in the field, click on the Submit button.

Login Page – II

Once you click the Submit button, you will be navigated to the following page.
This page contains the following components:

  • Password field – Enter your password associated with the email.
  • Log In Button – Click to login to Gateway.

Login

To login, follow these steps:

  1. Enter your Email as mentioned in the subsection Login Page – I.
  2. Enter your Password as mentioned in the subsection Login Page – II.
  3. Click the Log In button.

If your credentials are correct:

  • You will be redirected to the Home Page.
  • Your session will begin immediately.

You may experience various authentication workflows when logging into the Gateway application, each with specific resolution steps.

Security Best Practices

For your safety:

  • Never share your password
  • Log out after using shared devices
  • Always use secure networks

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues:

  • Clear your browser cache
  • Confirm your internet connectivity

If the problem persists:

  • Contact system administrator
  • Provide screenshots if possible
  • Mention the exact error message
